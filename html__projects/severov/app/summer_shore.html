«Summer Shore is geographically name of coastal area of White Sea, Onegian Peninsula, North of Arkhangelsk region.
That serie have the same name and dedicated to that place. Vitaly was born in Severodvinsk City which stood on start point of Summer Shore. Trought the travellings across the shore in few years, Vitaly explore the theme of personal memmories and influence of them on perception. Trough visual and the sequencee of pictures Vitaly makes the story of journey in timeless. In narrative he explore the flowing of time.
In a traditional form, he connects time and light and builds a narrative through a landscape of light. The movement of time is inextricably linked with light, observations of the sun and celestial bodies were the basis of clocks and calendars. Time inevitably moves forward, life is changeable in this stream and often doomed to fade. This series is a reflection on time and an attempt to look at time unlinearly»
